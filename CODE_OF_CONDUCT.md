# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want this project to be a welcoming, professional place for contributors and users.

Examples of positive behavior include:

- discussing technical decisions with evidence and respect;
- giving actionable feedback;
- respecting privacy, copyright, trademarks, and customer asset rights;
- accepting responsibility and correcting mistakes.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment, personal attacks, discrimination, or threats;
- publishing private information, credentials, customer images, or vulnerability details without permission;
- knowingly submitting unlicensed assets or code;
- impersonating OpenAI, a customer, or another contributor;
- sustained disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary.

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, releases, and other official project spaces.
